# Video Game Sales Analysis – 2017 Market Forecasting

This project was developed as part of a Data Analyst Bootcamp program.  
The objective is to analyze global video game sales data to identify patterns that determine commercial success and support strategic planning for 2017.

The analysis includes platform trends, genre performance, regional market differences, review impact assessment, and statistical hypothesis testing.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze platform lifecycle and identify growth vs decline trends
- Determine the most profitable genres
- Create regional market profiles (North America, Europe, Japan)
- Evaluate the relationship between review scores and sales
- Perform hypothesis testing on user ratings
- Provide business-oriented forecasting insights for 2017

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains historical sales data up to 2016 and includes:

- Game name
- Platform
- Release year
- Genre
- Regional sales (NA, EU, JP, Other)
- Critic scores
- User scores
- ESRB rating classification

Sales values are expressed in millions of USD.

---

## Key Insights

### Platform Trends
- Older consoles (PS3, Xbox 360) show declining sales.
- New-generation platforms (PS4, Xbox One) demonstrate stronger growth potential.
- Platform lifecycle is a critical forecasting factor.

### Regional Differences
- North America favors Xbox platforms.
- Europe shows stronger PlayStation presence.
- Japan strongly prefers handheld platforms such as 3DS.
- Market behavior varies significantly by region.

### Genre Performance
- Action dominates total global revenue.
- Shooter generates the highest average sales per title.
- Role-Playing performs particularly well in Japan.
- Puzzle and Strategy tend to generate lower commercial impact.

### Review Impact
- Critic scores show moderate positive correlation with sales.
- User scores show no meaningful linear correlation with sales.
- Professional reviews appear to have stronger commercial influence.

### Hypothesis Testing (α = 0.05)
- No significant difference in user ratings between Xbox One and PC.
- Significant difference in user ratings between Action and Sports genres.

## Conclusion

Commercial success in the video game market is primarily influenced by platform generation, regional preferences, and genre selection. 

For 2017 forecasting, strategic focus should prioritize:
- Growing platforms (PS4, Xbox One)
- High-performing genres (Action, Shooter)
- Region-specific marketing approaches

This project demonstrates practical application of data analysis, statistical testing, and business interpretation within a real-world market scenario.
